We are SkyFlux

We are committed to deliver high-quality
extrusion equioments

SkyFlux, founded in 1997, is a professional manufacturer and global exporter specializing in blown film extrusion equipment. Headquartered in Anhui, China, the company operates seven strategically located manufacturing facilities. With an annual production capacity exceeding 3,000 sets of high-quality plastic extrusion production lines and comprehensive equipment, SkyFlux serves customers across more than 120 countries and regions worldwide.

We operate advanced machining centers, precision assembly workshops, and dedicated R&D laboratories, supported by a highly skilled team of engineers specializing in Mechanical design, electrical systems, and process optimization. This strong technical foundation enables us to deliver high-performance, reliable, and innovative equipment tailored to our customers’ needs.

Our main products include: 7-layer and 8-layer co-extrusion blown film lines, along with key extrusion components - providing comprehensive solutions for the global packaging and film production industries.
